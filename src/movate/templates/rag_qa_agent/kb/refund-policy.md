# Refund Policy

## Annual subscriptions

Annual subscriptions are refundable within the first 14 days of
purchase, prorated based on the unused portion of the term. After
day 14, no refunds are issued; the customer can downgrade to a
lower tier at the next renewal but cannot recover paid funds for
the current term.

## Monthly subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but cancel-anytime. The
subscription stays active until the end of the paid month, then
drops to the free tier automatically.

## Refund processing time

Refunds are issued back to the original payment method within 5-7
business days of finance approval. Customers paying via ACH may
experience a 10-day delay due to bank processing timelines.

## Enterprise tier

Enterprise tier includes a 90-day money-back guarantee on the
first year only; subsequent renewals follow the standard 14-day
policy.
