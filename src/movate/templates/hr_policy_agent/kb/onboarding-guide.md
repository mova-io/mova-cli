# New Employee Onboarding Guide

**Applies to:** All new full-time employees
**Policy owner:** People Operations & IT
**Onboarding questions:** onboarding@company.com | Slack: #onboarding-help
**Benefits enrollment:** BambooHR portal (benefits.company.com)

---

## Before Your First Day

The company takes care of setup logistics before you arrive so you can hit the ground
running. Here's what to expect and what you need to do in the week before Day 1:

**From IT:**
- Your company-issued laptop will be shipped via **FedEx** and arrive **2 business days
  before your start date**. If it doesn't arrive by the evening before your start date,
  contact IT at it-help@company.com immediately.
- Included in the box: laptop, power adapter, and a welcome card with the IT onboarding
  hotline number.

**From HR (welcome email):**
- You will receive a welcome email 3–5 business days before your start date from
  hr-onboarding@company.com containing:
  - An **Okta account setup link** (expires in 72 hours — activate it immediately)
  - Instructions for completing your **I-9 employment eligibility verification** remotely
    via **Boundless** (must be completed before Day 1)
  - A link to the employee handbook (this document) in Notion
  - Your direct manager's contact information and a calendar invite for your Day 1 meetings

**Pre-start checklist:**
- [ ] Activate Okta account within 72 hours of receiving the link
- [ ] Complete I-9 verification via Boundless before start date
- [ ] Confirm laptop delivery; contact IT if it hasn't arrived 1 business day before start
- [ ] Review the employee handbook sections on PTO, benefits, and code of conduct
- [ ] Set up 1Password using the link sent in your Okta welcome email

## Day 1 Schedule

Day 1 is structured to ensure you have accounts, access, tools, and context before
the end of business. The schedule below assumes a standard 9am start; your manager
may adjust times slightly based on timezone.

**9:00 AM — IT Onboarding Call (30 minutes)**
Join the IT onboarding Zoom call (link in your welcome email). During this call, IT will:
- Verify your Okta and SSO access are working
- Walk you through installing required software: **Slack**, **Zoom**, **1Password**,
  **Notion**, and **Linear**
- Connect your laptop to the **company VPN** (Tailscale) and verify connectivity
- Grant access to your role-specific tools (GitHub org invite sent by your manager;
  other access requests submitted via IT ticket system)

**10:00 AM — HR Orientation (60 minutes)**
Your HR Business Partner will host a live 1:1 or small-group orientation covering:
- **Benefits enrollment walkthrough:** You have a **30-day window from your hire date**
  to elect health, dental, vision, 401k, and other benefits in BambooHR. Elections made
  outside this window require a qualifying life event. Benefits are effective on the
  **first day of the following month** after your hire date.
- **Payroll setup in Rippling:** Direct deposit information, tax withholding elections (W-4),
  pay schedule (bi-monthly), and pay stub access
- **PTO system walkthrough in Rippling:** How to request PTO, check your accrual balance,
  and read the PTO policy highlights
- **Key policies review:** Code of conduct, acceptable use, and data privacy policies

**12:00 PM — Lunch break**
Take a real break — there's no structured activity. Most new employees use this time
to explore the office, meet colleagues informally, or catch up on the Slack channels
they've been invited to.

**2:00 PM — Meeting with Direct Manager (60 minutes)**
Your first substantive conversation with your manager. Expect to discuss:
- Your role, team structure, and immediate priorities for Week 1
- How your manager prefers to communicate and what cadence of 1:1s to expect
- Any ongoing projects you'll be ramping into
- 30/60/90-day expectations (see below)

**Remainder of Day 1:**
- Accept Slack workspace invite and join the required channels (#general, #announcements,
  #onboarding, your team channel, and any project channels your manager adds you to)
- Introduce yourself in #general and #onboarding
- Review your team's Notion space and any pinned documentation
- Complete your IT access checklist in Linear (a ticket is auto-created for you)

## Week 1 Required Meetings

In addition to Day 1, your first week should include the following meetings. Your
manager will schedule these unless noted otherwise:

- **1:1 with direct manager** (recurring; Day 1 counts as the first session)
- **Team standup or sync introduction** — attend your team's regular standup and
  introduce yourself; your manager will tell you the cadence and Zoom link
- **Skip-level intro with your manager's manager** — a 30-minute coffee chat to
  introduce yourself and get broader context on the team and business unit
- **IT security training** — asynchronous, approximately **45 minutes**, available on
  the Learnerbly platform; **must be completed by EOD Friday of Week 1**

## 30 / 60 / 90-Day Goals

The company uses a structured ramp framework to set clear expectations and give you
measurable milestones during your first quarter.

**30-Day Goals:**
- Complete all required training courses
- Shadow at least 3 cross-functional meetings outside your immediate team
- Ship one small, low-risk contribution: a merged pull request, a published documentation
  update, or a completed analysis — something concrete you can point to
- Schedule introductory 1:1s with at least 5 teammates outside your direct team

**60-Day Goals:**
- Own at least one medium-scope project or feature from scoping through delivery or
  recommendation
- Present your work in a team sync, demo, or written summary to your immediate team
- Complete the 60-day onboarding experience feedback survey in **Culture Amp**
  (survey link emailed at Day 55)

**90-Day Goals:**
- Operate independently on your primary responsibilities with minimal direction on
  day-to-day tasks
- Complete your **first performance check-in** with your manager (structured conversation,
  not a formal review; documented in Rippling)
- Propose at least one concrete improvement to your team's processes, tooling, or
  documentation — and either implement it or submit it as a proposal in Linear

## Benefits Enrollment Details

Your **30-day benefits enrollment window** opens on your hire date and closes exactly
30 calendar days later. To enroll:

1. Log in to **BambooHR** (benefits.company.com) using your Okta SSO credentials
2. Navigate to Benefits → Open Enrollment
3. Select your health plan (Gold, Silver, or Bronze), add dependents if applicable,
   and elect dental and vision coverage
4. Enroll in or waive 401k contributions via the **Fidelity NetBenefits** link (also
   found in BambooHR)
5. If electing the Bronze health plan, set up your **HSA** through HealthEquity
   (link provided in BambooHR); if electing Gold or Silver, set up your **FSA**
6. Submit your elections — you will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours

**Important:** If you miss the 30-day window, you cannot enroll in or change benefits
until the next **Annual Open Enrollment** period (typically November), unless you
experience a **Qualifying Life Event** (marriage, birth, adoption, loss of other coverage).
Contact benefits@company.com if you believe you have a qualifying event.

Benefits elections are effective on the **first day of the month following your hire date**.
For example, if your hire date is June 10, coverage begins July 1.

## Equipment and System Access

- **Badge access:** Your office access badge is activated on Day 1 by the Workplace
  Experience team; pick it up at the front desk on Day 1
- **GitHub organization:** Invite sent by your manager on Day 1; accept via the email
  sent to your company email address
- **AWS access:** Available via **SAML SSO** through Okta; submit a request via the
  IT ticket system (Jira Service Management) for role-specific access; standard
  read-only access is granted within 1 business day, write/admin access requires a
  security review (typically 3–5 business days)
- **Admin-level access** to any system requires a security review approved by your
  manager, the Security team, and IT; submit your request at least 5 business days
  before you need the access
- **Slack:** You are added to company-wide channels automatically; ask your manager
  to add you to team and project channels on Day 1
