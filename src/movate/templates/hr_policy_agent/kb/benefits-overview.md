# Employee Benefits Overview

**Effective date:** January 1, 2025
**Enrollment portal:** BambooHR (benefits.company.com)
**Benefits questions:** benefits@company.com
**Policy owner:** Total Rewards & People Operations

---

## 1. Health Insurance

The company offers three health insurance plans through **Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS)**
for employees and their eligible dependents. The company covers **85% of the monthly
premium** for all three plans; employees pay the remaining 15%.

| Plan   | Employee Monthly Premium | Annual Deductible | Out-of-Pocket Maximum |
|--------|--------------------------|-------------------|-----------------------|
| Gold   | $0/month                 | $500              | $2,000                |
| Silver | $45/month                | $1,500            | $4,000                |
| Bronze | $120/month               | $3,000            | $6,500                |

**Gold Plan:** Best suited for employees who anticipate frequent medical care or have
ongoing prescriptions. Low deductible and the lowest out-of-pocket exposure.

**Silver Plan:** A balanced option for employees with moderate healthcare usage. The
monthly premium is modest and the deductible is manageable for most planned care needs.

**Bronze Plan:** Lowest monthly cost; best suited for healthy employees who rarely need
medical care. Pairs with an HSA (see Section 5) to offset the higher deductible. The
company contributes $500/year to the HSA for Bronze plan enrollees.

All three plans include prescription drug coverage, mental health parity, and preventive
care at 100% with no cost-sharing (annual physicals, recommended screenings, immunizations).
Coverage is effective on the **first day of the month following hire date** for new hires
who enroll during the 30-day new-hire enrollment window.

## 2. Dental Insurance

Dental coverage is provided through **Delta Dental PPO** and is available for employees
and eligible dependents.

- **Preventive care** (cleanings, exams, X-rays): **100% covered**, no cost-sharing,
  twice per year
- **Basic restorative** (fillings, simple extractions): **80% covered** after deductible
- **Major restorative** (crowns, root canals, bridges): **50% covered** after deductible
- **Annual maximum benefit:** $1,500 per covered person
- **Orthodontia:** Covered for dependent children under 19 at 50%, up to a $1,500
  lifetime maximum. **Not covered for adults.**

The annual deductible for dental is $50/individual, $150/family, and applies to basic
and major services only (not preventive). Delta Dental's network includes over 150,000
dentists nationwide; out-of-network visits are reimbursed at the in-network rate less 20%.

## 3. Vision Insurance

Vision coverage is provided through **VSP** (Vision Service Plan).

- **Eye exam:** One comprehensive exam per calendar year, $0 copay at a VSP network provider
- **Frames:** $150 allowance per calendar year toward frames; 20% discount on amounts
  exceeding the allowance
- **Lenses:** $0 copay for standard single-vision, bifocal, or trifocal lenses
- **Contact lenses (elective):** $0 copay for fitting and evaluation; $150 allowance
  toward contact lens supply per calendar year (in lieu of frames)
- **Contact lenses (medically necessary):** Covered in full with prior authorization

VSP network providers can be located at vsp.com. Out-of-network benefits are available
at reduced reimbursement levels ($45 for exam, $70 for frames, $105 for contacts).

## 4. 401(k) Retirement Plan

The company offers a **401(k) plan** administered by Fidelity. Key details:

- **Company match:** The company matches **4% of salary** on the first **8%** of salary
  contributed by the employee. To receive the full match, employees must contribute at
  least 8% of their salary.
- **Vesting schedule (cliff + graded hybrid):**
  - Year 1: 25% vested
  - Year 2: 50% vested
  - Year 3: 75% vested
  - Year 4: 100% vested (fully vested)
- **Employee contribution limits:** Subject to IRS annual limits ($23,000 for 2025;
  $30,500 for employees aged 50+)
- **Investment options:** 25+ funds including index funds, target-date funds, and
  actively managed funds; Fidelity BrokerageLink available for self-directed investors
- **Roth 401(k):** Available as an option; company match is always deposited as
  pre-tax regardless of employee's Roth/traditional election

Enrollment is available at any time; changes to contribution percentages take effect on
the first payroll of the following month.

## 5. Health Savings Account (HSA)

An HSA is available **exclusively to employees enrolled in the Bronze health plan**,
which is an IRS-qualifying High Deductible Health Plan (HDHP).

- **Company HSA contribution:** $500/year (deposited in January; prorated for mid-year hires)
- **Employee contribution limit (2025):** $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
- **HSA administrator:** HealthEquity
- Funds roll over year to year with no expiration; HSA is owned by the employee
  and is portable upon departure

---

## 6. Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

An FSA is available to employees enrolled in the **Gold or Silver health plans**.
(HSA and FSA cannot be combined; Bronze enrollees use the HSA instead.)

- **Annual contribution limit:** $2,850 (2025 IRS maximum)
- **Maximum rollover:** $610 of unused funds may roll to the following plan year;
  amounts above $610 are forfeited under the IRS "use-it-or-lose-it" rule
- **Eligible expenses:** Medical, dental, vision, and dependent care (separate
  Dependent Care FSA available; $5,000/year limit)
- **FSA administrator:** HealthEquity; debit card provided for direct payment

## 7. Equity Compensation

Full-time employees at Levels 3 and above are eligible for **Incentive Stock Options
(ISOs)** granted at time of hire.

- **Typical grant range:** 0.05%–0.5% of fully diluted shares, depending on level
  and role (exact grant amount is stated in the offer letter)
- **Vesting schedule:** 4-year vesting with a 1-year cliff; 25% vests at the 1-year
  anniversary, then monthly thereafter
- **Exercise window post-departure:** 90 days from the last day of employment for
  vested options; unvested options are forfeited immediately
- **Strike price:** Set at Fair Market Value (FMV) as determined by the most recent
  409A valuation on the grant date

## 8. Commuter Benefits

- **Pre-tax transit and parking:** Up to **$150/month** pre-tax for qualifying mass
  transit passes and parking expenses, administered through **Navia Benefit Solutions**
- **Bike commuter benefit:** $20/month reimbursement for documented bike commuting
  expenses (maintenance, lock, helmet); taxable benefit
- Enrollment and changes can be made monthly via the Navia portal

---

## 9. Life and Disability Insurance

- **Basic life insurance:** 2x annual base salary; **company-paid**; coverage begins
  on Day 1
- **Optional life insurance:** Employees may elect up to 5x annual salary; premiums
  are employee-paid and increase with age; medical underwriting required above 3x salary
- **Short-term disability (STD):** 60% of weekly earnings, up to $2,500/week, for
  up to 12 weeks; company-paid; 7-day elimination period
- **Long-term disability (LTD):** 60% of monthly salary, up to $10,000/month, after
  90-day elimination period; company-paid

## 10. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

The company provides an **Employee Assistance Program** through **Lyra Health** at no
cost to employees and their household members.

- **6 free counseling sessions per year** with a licensed therapist or coach; sessions
  are confidential and not reported to the company
- **24/7 crisis line** for urgent mental health support
- **Work-life services:** Financial coaching, legal consultation (30-minute session),
  child/elder care referrals, and digital mental health tools
- To access: Download the Lyra Health app or visit care.lyrahealth.com; use company
  code provided in your onboarding materials
